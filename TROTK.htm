About the Book

The Return of the King is the third and final volume of the epic novel The Lord of the Rings by J. R. R. Tolkien. following The Two Towers and The Return of the King. Is was published in 1955. The story begins in the kingdom of Gondor, which is soon to be attacked by the Dark Lord Sauron.
